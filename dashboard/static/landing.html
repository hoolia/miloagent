MILOAGENT

Autonomous AI Growth Agent

An AI agent that lives on Reddit, learns from every interaction, and grows your product organically. Self-learning, multi-platform, and 100% open source.

Star on GitHub Admin Dashboard

What Milo Does

A full growth engine that runs autonomously 24/7

🔍

Reddit Expert

Scans subreddits, finds relevant conversations, and writes human-like comments that add genuine value while naturally promoting your product.

🧠

Self-Learning

A/B tests its own prompts, evolves strategies, and optimizes keyword weights based on real engagement data. Gets smarter every cycle.

🌐

Multi-Platform

Operates across Reddit, Twitter/X, and Telegram simultaneously. Manages conversations, relationships, and community presence on each platform.

💰

Zero Cost LLMs

Runs on free-tier LLMs: Groq (Llama 3.3 70B), Google Gemini (2.0 Flash), and local Ollama models. No API bills, no subscriptions.

🏘️

Community Builder

Creates and moderates your own subreddits. Automated setup, rules, flairs, AutoModerator, and weekly content scheduling.

🛡️

Safety First

Rate limiting, ban detection, circuit breakers, account rotation, and smart cooldowns. Designed to stay under Reddit's radar.
